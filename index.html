FAQ

Questions about KidWay.

What is KidWay?

KidWay is a MoviSafe digital platform for school transport monitoring, route visibility, automatic alerts and digital attendance records.

Who is the solution designed for?

It is designed for parents, independent school transport drivers and school mobility companies operating daily routes in urban areas such as Lima Metropolitana.

What problem does KidWay solve?

It reduces uncertainty for parents and lowers risky manual communication for drivers by centralizing route status, location and notifications.

How does KidWay help drivers?

It helps drivers reduce waiting time, avoid repeated messages and manage student pickup or arrival information more efficiently.
